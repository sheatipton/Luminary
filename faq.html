2. Functionality

What is a vendor account?

Luminary features a large assortment of books available for online purchase and in-store purchase. Luminary allows anyone to sign up for a free Vendor Account. Vendors are able to add, update, and remove books. Vendors have access to end of day sales reports and inventory notice reports.

Is my data secure?

Luminary will never give out your email address or personal information. Luminary uses a secure login and checkout to protect your identity and information.

3. Payments

What are the available payment options?

Currently we support cash in store, and credit & debit card for online purchases.

What is the return policy?

To return an item within 30 days of purchase simply bring the item and your cash register receipt to your local store for a refund to your original form of payment. Please contact your local store for more details.
